Our Story

Proud to be running Chalet l'Aiglon we are Freja, Jonny and Biscuit the dog. We all share a passion for the mountains, skiing and most importantly good food and wine (yes, even Biscuit!)

Chalet l'Aiglon is also our home, so you’ll see plenty of us during your stay and we're always happy to help. We have been living in La Rosière for a few years now and so we’ve got plenty of insider knowledge of the village and ski area to share with you.

We can’t wait to welcome you to our favourite place and share our love of La Rosière with you.

We’re on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram so follow us to see what we’re doing at the moment.
