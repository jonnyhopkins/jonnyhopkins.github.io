About The Chalet

The chalet will be furnished with a modern but cosy feel with large windows, and a cosy log burning fire - traditional wood will complement a modern feel.

The open-plan living and dining area is where you can relax, with a cosy sitting area and two large dining tables where you’ll join your fellow guests for breakfast and dinner.

There are three different types of bedrooms - two standard double rooms with ensuite shower room, two large double rooms based at the top of the chalet also with ensuites and a family room with separated double and twin rooms and a shared bathroom.

For a precise idea of the layout of our chalet, you can consult our floor plans below.

The spa has a four person hammam steam room and a hot tub which overlooks the Tarentaise valley. It’s open each afternoon, giving you the chance to unwind after a hard day of skiing.

We also have a massage room, with treatments available on request and subject to availability.
