About The Chalet

The chalet is furnished with a modern but cosy feel with large windows, and a cosy log burning fire - traditional wood complements a modern feel.

The open-plan living and dining area is where you can relax, with a comfy sitting area and two large dining tables where you’ll join your fellow guests for breakfast and dinner.

The spa has a four person hammam steam room and a hot tub which overlooks the Tarentaise valley. It’s open each afternoon, giving you the chance to unwind after a hard day of skiing.

We also have a massage room, with treatments available on request and subject to availability.
