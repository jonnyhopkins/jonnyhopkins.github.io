About The Chalet

The chalet will be furnished with a modern but cosy feel with large windows, high ceilings and cosy log burning fires - hints of traditional wood and red will be complemented with modern designs. The main communal area is a large living-dining space, where you’ll find plenty of plush seating to sink into.

There are 3 different types of bedrooms - 2 standard double rooms with ensuite shower room, 2 large double rooms based at the top of the chalet also with ensuites and a family suite with a twin room and a double room which share a bathroom.

The spa in the chalet has a Hammam steam room and a hot tub which overlooks the Tarentaise valley. These will be open for use everyday from 4pm to 8pm. There is also a massage room and we’ll be able to book massages in the chalet on request with the wonderful xsupplierx.
