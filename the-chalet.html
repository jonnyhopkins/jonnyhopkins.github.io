About The Chalet

The chalet is furnished with a modern but cosy feel with large windows, and a cosy log burning fire - traditional wood complements a modern feel.

The open-plan living and dining area is where you can relax, with a comfy sitting area and two large dining tables where you’ll join your fellow guests for breakfast and dinner.

Previous Next

Comfort Rooms

On the top floor we have our comfort rooms - the most spacious in the chalet. They both have en-suite shower rooms and balconies, with beds configurable as doubles or twins.

Previous Next

Standard Rooms

On the first floor we have our two standard rooms with en-suite shower rooms. South-facing with a shared balcony giving great views of the valley, both rooms can be configured as either a double or a twin.

Previous Next

Family Suite

Alongside our two standard rooms is our family room with a comfortable room at the front of the chalet with a shared balcony, and a smaller room at the back - perfect for your children. The suite has a private bathroom with separate WC, and each room can be made up as either doubles or twins.

Previous Next

Spa

The spa has a four person hammam steam room and a hot tub which overlooks the Tarentaise valley. It’s open each afternoon, giving you the chance to unwind after a hard day of skiing.

We also have a massage room, with treatments available on request and subject to availability.
