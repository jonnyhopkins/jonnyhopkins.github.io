Finding Us

Check your sat nav! The Col du Petit St. Bernard between Italy and France is closed in winter.

Approaching from Chambery, follow signs for Albertville.

Once you have reached Albertville continue on the main road (RN90), following signs for Bourg St. Maurice.

At Bourg St. Maurice, follow signs for La Rosière (D1090).

At the La Rosière entrance archway, head straight on at the rounabout.

Take the first left, just after the garage on the right.

Continue 150m, we're on the right!

Connections

By Air

The nearest airports are Geneva, Grenoble, Lyon and Chambery.

Need an airport transfer? Chalet l'Aiglon customers recieve a 5% discount from 3sixty Airport Transfers.

By Rail

The nearest train station is in Bourg St. Maurice.

Need a lift from the station? Give us a ring and we'll help you arrange something.
