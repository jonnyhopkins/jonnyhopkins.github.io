Welcome to Chalet Aiglon: a brand-new chalet with five luxury bedrooms, coming to La Rosière in winter 2018-2019.

Stunning Views

Based in the sun soaked village of La Rosière in the Tarentaise Valley, our boutique chalet is being set up to provide high-quality, flexible accommodation with a friendly and personal service.

A newly renovated and extended chalet with panoramic views, Chalet Aiglon aims to provide the perfect retreat after a day out on the mountain.

Our southwest-facing balconies offer excellent views across the valley, so you’ll be able to enjoy them until the last rays of the sunset disappear behind the mountains.

Five Spacious Rooms

Using sympathetic materials such as wood and stone, the chalet aims to bring a modern feel to the traditional chalet style. It’s large windows will let in beautiful alpine sunshine, leaving the rooms light and airy.

Each of the five luxurious bedrooms will have its own unique style, balcony and en suite bathroom. Downstairs each room has a private, heated ski locker.

Relax, We've Got You Covered

We also have a spa area complete with massage room, hammam and a hot tub that overlooks our stunning views of the Tarentaise valley.

Breakfast will be available every morning, with a continental spread and a different hot option each day. When you return from skiing, some afternoon treats will be waiting for you, and a three course set menu will be on offer six nights a week for those who'd like to dine in.

Space To Ski

For skiing, our chalet is located 200m from two resort bus stops, so you’ll never have to trudge up the hill in ski boots.

The Espace San Bernardo offers plenty for all ages and abilities from quiet greens and blues to vertigo inducing blacks in the Italian domaine to endless possibilities for adventure further afield. La Rosière is one of the only French resorts able to offer heliskiing drops thanks to our Italian neighbours.
